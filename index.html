Data Deletion Request

Your privacy is our priority. This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data from the Earzbook app.

How to Delete Your Data

We provide two ways for you to request the deletion of your data, fulfilling the requirements of the Google Play Store.

Option 1: In-App Account Deletion (Recommended)

  • Step 1: Open the Earzbook app and navigate to the 'Settings' screen.
  • Step 2: At the bottom of the Settings screen, tap on the "Delete Account" button.
  • Step 3: A confirmation prompt will appear to ensure the action is intentional. Confirming this will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Option 2: Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request data deletion by sending an email to our support team.

  • Step 1: Compose an email from the address associated with your Earzbook account.
  • Step 2: Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  • Step 3: In the body of the email, please state that you wish to delete your account and all associated data.
  • Step 4: Send the email to the address below. Our team will process your request and confirm via email once the deletion is complete.

For all data deletion requests via email, please contact us at:

contact@earzbook.com
